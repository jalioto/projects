# Audio Files for One Punch Man Game

This folder should contain the following audio files for the game:

## Required Files:

### 1. background-music.mp3
- **Type**: Looping background music
- **Style**: Energetic, heroic, anime-inspired
- **Duration**: 2-4 minutes (will loop)
- **Volume**: Should be mixed at moderate levels
- **Suggestions**: 
  - Epic orchestral music
  - Rock/metal with heroic themes
  - Anime-style battle music
  - Upbeat electronic music

### 2. punch.mp3
- **Type**: Sound effect for punches
- **Style**: Powerful impact sound
- **Duration**: 0.5-1 second
- **Suggestions**:
  - Deep "whoosh" + impact sound
  - Martial arts punch sound
  - Comic book "POW" style effect

### 3. explosion.mp3
- **Type**: Sound effect for enemy explosions
- **Style**: Dramatic explosion
- **Duration**: 1-2 seconds
- **Suggestions**:
  - Cartoon explosion sound
  - Fireworks burst
  - Action movie explosion (short)

## Music Style Recommendations:

For a One Punch Man game, the music should capture:
- **Heroic Energy**: Uplifting and powerful
- **Anime Aesthetic**: Japanese animation style music
- **Action-Packed**: Fast tempo, driving rhythm
- **Slightly Comedic**: Not too serious (matches the anime's tone)

## Where to Find Music:

### Free Options:
- **Freesound.org**: Free sound effects and music
- **Zapsplat**: Free with registration
- **YouTube Audio Library**: Royalty-free music
- **OpenGameArt.org**: Game-specific audio

### Paid Options:
- **AudioJungle**: Professional game music
- **Pond5**: High-quality audio tracks
- **Artlist**: Subscription-based music library

### DIY Options:
- **GarageBand** (Mac): Create simple tracks
- **Audacity**: Free audio editing
- **LMMS**: Free music production software

## File Format Notes:
- Use MP3 format for best browser compatibility
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 5MB each)
- Background music can be 128kbps quality
- Sound effects can be 96kbps quality

## Implementation:
The game will automatically load and play these files when:
- Background music starts when the game begins
- Punch sound plays when you attack
- Explosion sound plays when enemies are destroyed
- Music can be toggled on/off with the music button
